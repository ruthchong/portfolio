About Me

Ruth Chong

Project Planner | Interior Designer | Developer

I have my own experienced team, providing expert consultancy in interior design, the F&B industry, and web development.

With a decade of experience in management across diverse industries, I have consistently focused on leadership and organizational oversight.
My career began in Malaysia's prominent entertainment sector as a Database Engineer, where I managed and synchronized multimedia servers across multiple branches.
I played a pivotal role in enhancing the company's media database through market research, establishing it as a leader in multimedia content provision.
Transitioning into interior design and construction, I expanded my skill set to independently create floor plans and 3D renderings. From understanding client requirements and designing proposals to overseeing project execution, I navigated the complexities of the renovation industry with proficiency.
Before the pandemic, I ventured into entrepreneurship by founding a pet product manufacturing company, where I served as Executive Director. I led product development, strategic planning, and market expansion initiatives.
Throughout these diverse roles, my core strength in management has been essential to my success, enabling seamless transitions and effective leadership across various professional environments.
