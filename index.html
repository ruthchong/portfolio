With a decade of experience in management across diverse industries, I have consistently focused on leadership and organizational oversight.

Transitioning into interior design and construction, I expanded my skill set to independently create floor plans and 3D renderings. Throughout these diverse roles, my core strength in management has been essential to my success, enabling seamless transitions and effective leadership across various professional environments.
